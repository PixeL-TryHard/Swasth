Anganwadi is a vital community-based program in India that aims to provide a comprehensive package of services to children under six years of age, pregnant women, and lactating mothers. It is a unique platform that focuses on early childhood care and development, nutrition, and health-related services, thereby playing a crucial role in shaping the future of our nation.
